  • Web Development

    We at Outland Dynamics are very familiar with developing/designing websites, in fact, the website you're currently on was created by one of our web designers.     As you can imagine, developing mobile apps isn't easy. That's probably why you're looking into hiring someone.     The reason that we started Outland Dynamics originally was to develop video games. With that being said, it's something we are very passionate about. Price Structure for Development

There is no set price to develop anything listed above. We are not able to do this because there are many different variables involved. Such as: If you want us to host your website or how fast you need a project done, If we're making a game, do you need us to buy assets or can you provide your own? Like I said, there are a lot of different factors involved. Because of this, we will want you to contact us and specify what you would like done. Specify things like: The time frame and what exactly you want done. Anything further can be discussed through our email or you can try to contact us on our Outland Dynamics Discord server.
